In outer space one lone dare devil dodges asteroids that are on fire.

Use the arrows keys to control the space craft, press space to activate your power bomb.
Press L to restart the game if you die.
Red capsules are power ups run into them to gain a power bomb; you may only have one power bomb at a time.
If a golden cube spawns run into it to win the game.
For best gameplay experience play in 1024x768.
